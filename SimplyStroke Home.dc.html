The problem with golf apps

Most scoring apps make you do the one thing you came here to escape: keep track.

Menus, handicaps, GPS overlays, ads, sign-ups. By the third hole you've lost count and put the phone away. SimplyStroke throws it all out and keeps the single job: count this stroke.
